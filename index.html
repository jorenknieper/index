About me

My name is Joren Knieper. My passion is recording and mixing music. I started recording music on a 2-channel interface when I was 17 years old. When I got 19 I invested with a good friend of mine Lennert Baerts in a 16 channel interface, microphones and other recording gear.
Nowadays, we're using all the gear we've managed to get in a few month to record music or to have several extra options live.

Student IT

I'm following a bachelor degree in IT (System and Networking). I love the way networks are integrated nowadays in our life. Following this program is giving me more insight of how this is and can be done.

Recording

I love recording music. One of my biggest dreams is to combine live engineering and studio engineering. Two beautiful and different worlds, with a lot of different techniques but if you combine the two of them you will see that they will blend and two worlds will become one.
