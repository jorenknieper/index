About me

My Name is Joren Knieper. I'm a student IT (System And Networks) with a passion for recording and mixing music. Recording and mixing music started out as a hobby. I always wanted to record music so I got an interface with 2 pre-amps and recorded a lot of songs in my bedroom. This was my foundation. Later on I got to know a Sound Engineer from Maaseik (Erwin Dirckx - Grindstone Recordings) who taught me a lot techniques in a live environment. From that moment I knew I wanted to become a Professional Live Sound Engineer. Nowadays I'm trying my best to combine professional work with getting a bachelors degree in IT.

Student IT

I'm following a bachelors degree in IT (System and Networks). I love the way Networks are integrated nowadays in our life and how protocols function in the OSI-model. My favorite topics are Cisco and Security. Following this program is giving me more insight of how everything can be connected and will soon be handy in live sound engineering. One of the things on my to do list are researching and studying the MADI and DANTE protocol.

Recording - mix engineer

I love recording music. One of my biggest dreams is to combine Live sound engineering with Studio (mix) engineering. Music is so sensitive in every environment. I want to create the best environment for the musician live and in the studio. The reason I want to become a sound engineer is that I want to translate the song with good microphones - a good set of speakers, so the audience can understand what the songs is about, what the music is trying to say, to create feelings. I want the musician to rely on the sound engineer. My vision is that the musicians should always be able to wholeheartedly trust it's sound enigneer. My job is purely to make sure the music, the song, gets perfectly translated as the musician(s) wanted it to sound in a studio or in a live environment.
