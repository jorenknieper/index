Engineering on your demand

"As a Mix Engineer I'm trying to translate what the musician is performing on stage or in the studio. My tools for this translation is a couple of cables, microphones and a mixing console. I'm trying do discover what you on stage want to bring over to the audience. For me the first step is to meet the band, have a chat about the music and try to find out what their believes and disbelieves are. That way I know what the performer wants, then my only job is to translate it well enough for the people in the venue to understand what the performer is trying to say."

About myself

My Name is Joren Knieper. I'm 21 years old and I've a passion for music (engineering side) and IT.

Mixing and Recording Music

My first experience recording was recording my own songs that can still be found on Soundcloud. After a few years of working in the live business and trying to become a Live Sound Engineer my passion for recording never went away. Nowadays I'm also recording Live Shows and bands in the studio.

Student IT

I'm a 3rd year student IT - System and Networks at Hogeschool PXL in Belgium. My passion for computers, system and networks started when I was a little kid. Nowadays I'm lucky enough to study how everything in this world is connected and how systems work. Studying and researching in the MADI and DANTE network protocol is one of my top things on my bucketlist.

Eager, passioned, excited and willing to make a difference

As Mix Engineer you are the one who's fully in-control and all responsibility to make sure the show will go right. I think that only, if you are fully committed to the show, band or venue you will succeed. Otherwise it is "just" another band who's playing in "just another venue". This is what you will never hear come out of my mouth. Every gig and every venue is special!
