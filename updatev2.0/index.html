Engineering on your demand

"As a Mix Engineer I'm trying to translate what the musician is performing on stage or in the studio. My tools for this translation is a couple of cables, microphones and a mixing console. I'm trying do discover what you as a musician on stage want to bring over to the audience. For me the first step is to meet the band, have a chat about the music and try to find out what their believes and disbelieves are. That way I know what the performer wants, then my only job is to translate it well enough for the people in the venue to understand what the performer is trying to say."
